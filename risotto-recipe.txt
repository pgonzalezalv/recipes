Ingredients:
 - 1 tablespoon of olive oil
 - 2 nice onions, chopped
 - 1 clove garlic, chopped
 - 1 red pepper, chopped
 - 1 cup of risotto rice
 - 1/4 cup of white wine
 - 3 tomatoes, chopped
 - 1 bouillon cube mixed with 1 cup of water
 - 50 grams of Parmesan

Directions:
 1. In a medium saucepan, add the olive oil and preheat the pan.
 2. When the oil is warm, add the onions, the garlic and the pepper.
 3. Once they are cooked, add the rice and the wine. Let the rice absorb the
    liquid.
 4. Add the water with the dissolved bouillon cube.
 5. Cook and stir for 16 minutes
 6. Add the Parmesan and let the risotto rest for 5 to 10 minutes.
 7. Enjoy the meal! ;)
